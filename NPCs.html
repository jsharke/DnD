Citizens of Rozarria

A middle aged man and his young blonde daughter that the heroes befriended in the small village of Voltera. After the fall of Voltera, Vinny suggested the group came with him to Rozarria to look for work as his cousin, Hinno, works as a cobblemaker by the port. During the trip, Dabdoob learned that Vinny's wife had been taken by half-orcs.
