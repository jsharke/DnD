Citizens of Rozarria

A middle aged human man and his young blonde daughter that the heroes befriended in the small village of Voltera. After the fall of Voltera, the party accompagnied them to the land of Rozarria. During the trip, the party learned that Vinny's wife had been taken by half-orcs a years prior.
